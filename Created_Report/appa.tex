    \textbf{Authors:} Khushi Yadav, Chinmay Nanda, Ayush Raj Chauhan
    
    \textbf{Supervisor:} Ms. Vishakha Chauhan
    
    \textbf{Conference:} [Conference Name, if applicable]
    
    \textbf{Year:} 2026
    
    \textbf{Status:} [Published/Accepted/Under Review]
    
    \textbf{Abstract:} This paper introduces ShadeAI, an end-to-end pipeline for automated skin-tone classification and foundation-shade recommendation. Leveraging classical computer vision and perceptual colour science rather than deep learning, the system achieves a mean colour difference ($\Delta E$) of 1.361 — 49.6\% below published research baselines — while operating in a zero-shot, CPU-only configuration. Classification follows the inclusive ten-class Monk Skin Tone scale, and recommendations draw from a curated database of 187 foundation shades spanning 14 major brands.
\end{enumerate}
